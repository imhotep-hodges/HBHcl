My Story

Since graduating from Harford Technical High School, I have been in the Salon industry for over 20 years. I love helping my clients look as beautiful on the outside as they are on the inside. When it comes to style, the possibilities are endless, but my specialties are: all aspects of Color, Razor Cutting, Short Hair, and Bobs.

I have attended Hair Shows in New York, DC, and New Orleans where I’ve learned techniques from the top Hair Stylists in the world. I also regularly attend Hair Design skills courses in order to keep up with the latest trends.

My Chair is located at With Love Beauty Bar & Studios in Bel Air, Maryland. Schedule an appointment today!

Haircuts

Hair is shaped and texturized to deliver the desired look, enhance face shape, and highlight features. A shampoo and scalp massage is included with all haircut services.

Highlights, Color, and Style

Touch up regrowth and add dimension with a partial highlight around the face and crown area. Includes gloss, shampoo, scalp massage, cut and style. Price varies upon length and thickness.

Balayage

Balayage is a free hand technique of applying highlights and usually does not use foil to separate the hair. Balayage creates a natural gradation of lightness towards the ends. The result is the look of summers spent at the beach or the fresh, unintentionally perfect highlights on a child. This natural looking highlighting technique is not just for blondes, but is actually used on all shades of hair to add a soft sun-kissed dimension.

Bombshell Blowout

Using high quality volumizing products, you’ll leave feeling like a model with this bouncy, voluminous blowout. Includes shampoo, scalp massage and blow dry.

Consultation

New guests are required to book a consultation before any coloring service to discuss pricing, desired look and timing. Please bring 3 inspiration photos of the looks you are going for as they help me better understand your hair goals. This can also be done on a video call.
